AI ROADMAP
Is This Roadmap Useful for Future AGI and ASI?
▼

Short answer: Absolutely — but with important caveats.

The roadmap covers the full spectrum of current AI engineering: from the mathematical foundations of deep learning to cutting-edge specializations like hardware kernels, robotics, and bio-AI. It emphasizes critical areas like safety, alignment, and evaluation-driven development — all of which will be essential if and when we build Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) or Artificial Superintelligence (ASI).

AGI and ASI remain hypothetical. No one knows exactly what architectures or breakthroughs will lead to general intelligence. That said, the skills from this roadmap will position you to understand, build, and control future systems.

How This Roadmap Prepares You for AGI/ASI

🧠

1. Deep Understanding of Current Paradigms

AGI will likely evolve from today's large language models, multimodal systems, and agents. By mastering transformers, scaling laws, RAG, fine-tuning, and multimodality, you understand the building blocks that any future general intelligence might incorporate.

🛡️

2. Safety & Alignment — The "Trust" Layer

This roadmap dedicates a full phase to Constitutional AI, red teaming, prompt injection defense, and evaluation. As models become more powerful, alignment becomes the central challenge. Your hands-on experience will be invaluable.

⚡

3. Hardware & Optimization — The "Speed" Layer

AGI/ASI will require enormous computational efficiency. Your knowledge of CUDA kernels, speculative decoding, FlashAttention, and chip-specific optimizations (Groq, Cerebras) prepares you to make future systems run faster and cheaper.

🤖

4. Embodied AI & Robotics

Your experience with end-to-end transformer-based robotics and simulation will be directly applicable. Many believe true general intelligence must be grounded in physical experience through real-world interaction.

🔬

5. Bio-AI & Scientific Discovery

Your exposure to AlphaFold, protein language models, and generative biology gives you a foothold in one of the most promising frontiers where AGI may first emerge or be applied.

🧩

6. Agentic Systems & Long-Term Memory

The roadmap's focus on agents, tool use, and long-term memory architectures (generative agents, MCP) mirrors the kind of autonomy and persistence that AGI will need to operate effectively.

What's Missing? (The Unknown Unknowns)

No roadmap can predict the future. AGI may require:

  • New paradigms beyond deep learning (neurosymbolic AI, cognitive architectures, or something not yet imagined).
  • Radically different hardware (quantum computing, neuromorphic chips).
  • Breakthroughs in understanding intelligence itself (neuroscience, cognitive science).

However, the foundational skills you gain — mathematical maturity, software engineering, research reading, and a safety mindset — will allow you to adapt and contribute no matter what form AGI takes.

The Bottom Line

This roadmap builds the mental models and technical depth required to tackle the hardest problems in AI. If AGI is developed in your lifetime, you'll be among the engineers who understand its internals, can identify risks, and can steer it toward beneficial outcomes.

"It's arguably the best preparation available right now. It turns you from a consumer of AI into a builder and steward of the technology that may one day lead to general intelligence."

Keep learning, keep building, and stay curious. The future is yours to shape.

📊 Technical Analysis (Professional Level)

📐

Market Structure Analysis

HH/HL = Bullish. LH/LL = Bearish. Wait for structure break confirmation before reversing bias. Break of Structure (BOS) and Change of Character (CHoCH) signal trend transitions.

🕯️

Price Action Methods

Key patterns: Pin Bar (rejection), Engulfing (reversal), Inside Bar (consolidation/breakout), Doji (indecision). Identify S/R → wait for pattern → enter on confirmation → stop beyond extreme.

🎯

Supply & Demand Zones

Institutions think in zones where orders cluster — not lines. Supply Zone: sellers overwhelmed buyers. Demand Zone: buyers overwhelmed sellers. Fresh zones are more powerful than tested zones.

💧

Liquidity & Stop Hunts

Institutions push price to grab stops before reversing. Never place stops at exact swing points. Wait for the stop hunt, then enter in the opposite direction for high-probability setups.

📦

Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

Order Blocks: Last candle before impulsive move. Fair Value Gaps (FVG): Price inefficiencies where price may return. OTE: Confluence of FVG + Order Block + Fibonacci for ultra-high-probability entries.

🔢

Volume & Multi-Timeframe

Rising price + Rising volume = Healthy trend. Rising price + Falling volume = Exhaustion warning. Use 3 timeframes: HTF for direction, MTF for setup, LTF for entry precision.

🛡️ Risk Management — Hedge Fund Methods

The Golden Rule

Never risk more than you can afford to lose. Professionals risk 0.5% to 2% per trade.

Drawdown Rules

  • Down 3% in a day → Stop trading immediately
  • Down 6% in a week → Take 3 days off, review
  • Down 10% in a month → Full strategy review
  • Down 20% from peak → System audit, back to demo

Capital Preservation

  • Every position has a stop at entry
  • Move stop to breakeven after 1R profit
  • Decrease size during drawdowns
  • Never double down to recover losses
  • Hold 20–50% cash in uncertain markets

⚙️ Professional Trading Strategies

📈

Trend Pullback

Enter when price pulls back to 20 EMA with lower volume, forms bullish reversal candle, RSI 40–60. Stop below pullback low. Target: previous swing high (1.5R), then 3R with trailing stop.

💥

Range Breakout

Wait for volume >1.5× average break above resistance. Conservative: wait for pullback to broken level. Stop inside range. Target = measured move (range height).

↩️

Mean Reversion

RSI <30 + price below lower Bollinger Band + bullish divergence + volume spike on down day. Enter on first green candle. Targets: middle BB → upper BB.

🚀

Gap and Go

Gap up >3% on volume >2× average, breakaway gap type, holds above gap for 30–60 min. Enter above first 30-min high. Stop below gap level. Trail after 2R.

🧠 Trader Psychology — 5 Major Traps

🔄

1. Overtrading

Symptom: trading without setup conviction. Rule: max 3 trades/day. Require 10/10 conviction. Close platform after limit hit.

😰

2. FOMO

Chasing moves after they've started. Mantra: "There will always be another trade. The market pays patience." Accept missing moves.

😡

3. Revenge Trading

Rule: One loss = stop for the day. Review journal. Resume tomorrow with normal size. Never increase size after losses.

😟

4. Emotional Decisions

Framework: "Plan the trade, trade the plan." Predefine exact entry, stop, and target. Automate what you can. Emotion has no place in execution.

😴

5. Lack of Discipline

System: Treat trading as a business with set hours and procedures. Create real consequences for rule breaks. Have an accountability partner.
